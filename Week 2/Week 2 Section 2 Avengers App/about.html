About the Avengers

The Avengers are a fictional team of superheroes appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The team made its first appearance in The Avengers #1 (Sept. 1963), created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist/co-plotter Jack Kirby.


The Avengers are a team of superheroes that includes THOR, HULK, IRON MAN, CAPTAIN AMERICA, BLACK WIDOW, and HAWKEYE. They are known for their teamwork and ability to work together


to defeat powerful villains and protect the world from threats.


They have appeared in various comic book series, animated television shows, and live-action films, becoming one of the most popular superhero teams in popular culture.
